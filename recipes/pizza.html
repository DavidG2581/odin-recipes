Pizza

Home
  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  1. Preheat the pizza stone (or pizza pan or baking sheet)
  2. Divide the dough into two balls
  3. Prep the toppings
  4. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  5. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  6. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  7. Bake pizza in the 475°F oven, one at a time, until the crust is browned and the cheese is golden, about 10 to 15 minutes. If you want, toward the end of the cooking time you can sprinkle on a little more cheese
